Cottage maintenance

 

Winter

1.      Place the marker at the well head to warn the snow plow

2.      In December pour two containers of anti freeze into the laundry tub and flush with plenty of water

3.      In January pour two containers of anti freeze into the laundry tub and flush with plenty of water

4.      In February pour two containers of anti freeze into the laundry tub and flush with plenty of water

5.      At the start of a cold spell turn heat up to 20 degrees in ping-pong room and work shop and close the door to the hallway.  If it’s a severe cold spell turn on a trickle of water in the laundry tub and make sure nothing can fall into the tub and block the drain. Reverse when the cold spell is over

6.      Fill green container in the outdoor storage area with kindling

7.      Clean sand filter (see instructions by the hot water tank)

 

Spring

8.      Launch boats

9.      Remove ropes that secure the dock

10.  Remove the chains and replace the bolts that connect the gangway to the dock

11.  Set up patio furniture

12.  Remove the marker by the well head

13.  Put out outdoor mats

14.  Sweep / clean patios

15.  Fill green container in the outdoor storage area with kindling

16.  Clean sand filter (see instructions by the hot water tank)

 

Summer

17.  Fill green container in the outdoor storage area with kindling

18.  Clean sand filter (see instructions by the hot water tank)

 

Fall

19.  Take out boats and store

20.  Secure the dock with ropes from corners to land

21.  Remove the bolts that connect the gangway to the dock and replace with chains

22.  Sweep / clean patios

23.  Turn patio table upside down before snowfall

24.  Store outdoor mats in the outdoor storage area

25.  Fill green container in the outdoor storage area with kindling

26.  Clean sand filter (see instructions by the hot water tank)
